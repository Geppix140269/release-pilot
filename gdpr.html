2. Your Rights (Articles 15-22 GDPR)

Right to Access (Article 15)

  • Request a copy of all your personal data
  • Know how we process it
  • Free of charge (first request)
  • Response within 30 days

Right to Rectification (Article 16)

  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Complete incomplete data
  • Immediate update in our systems

Right to Erasure (Article 17)

  • Delete all your personal data
  • Remove from backups within 30 days
  • Exceptions: Legal obligations, tax records

Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)

  • Limit how we use your data
  • Pause processing during disputes
  • Maintain data but not use it

Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

  • Export your data in machine-readable format
  • Transfer to another service
  • JSON or CSV format available

Right to Object (Article 21)

  • Object to marketing communications
  • Object to profiling
  • Object to legitimate interests processing
