Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 26, 2025

Simple Version: We use essential cookies and analytics. No tracking cookies. You can disable them if you want.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide basic functionality and understand how you use our service.

2. How We Use Cookies

Essential Cookies

  • Session Management: Keep you logged in
  • Security: Protect against CSRF attacks
  • Preferences: Remember your settings

Analytics Cookies

  • Usage Tracking: Page views and navigation
  • Performance: Site speed and errors
  • Improvements: Understanding user behavior

We DON'T Use

  • Advertising cookies
  • Third-party tracking cookies
  • Social media cookies
  • Retargeting cookies

4. Third-Party Cookies

Stripe Payment Processing

When you make a payment, Stripe may set cookies for:

  • Fraud prevention
  • Payment processing
  • Security verification

See Stripe's Cookie Policy

GitHub/Azure DevOps OAuth

When you authenticate, these services may set cookies for:

  • Authentication tokens
  • Session management

5. Managing Cookies

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Consequences of Disabling Cookies

  • Essential cookies disabled: Can't log in or use the service
  • Analytics cookies disabled: No impact on functionality
  • All cookies disabled: Website may not work properly
